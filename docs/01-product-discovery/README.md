# 01 — Product Discovery

| | |
|---|---|
| **Phase** | Product Discovery |
| **Why this phase exists** | To establish *why* Cluely exists — its vision, objectives, scope, and boundaries — before any requirement or design work. It anchors every downstream decision. |
| **Owner** | Product Owner (reviewer: Business Analyst). |

## Purpose
Capture the product's reason for being: vision, business objectives, stakeholders, scope and
out-of-scope, assumptions, constraints, risks, and success criteria.

## Scope
The business framing of the product. Not detailed requirements (that is Business Analysis), not
mechanics detail (that is Business Rules).

## Documents
| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|----------|---------|
| 01 | [Business Requirements (BRD)](01-business-requirements.md) | Vision, objectives, stakeholders, scope/out-of-scope, assumptions, constraints, risks, success criteria, business glossary. |

## Dependencies
- **Input:** the product idea and stakeholder intent.
- **Output:** an approved BRD that constrains all later phases.

## Entry Criteria
- A product concept and stakeholders exist.

## Exit Criteria
- BRD approved; scope and out-of-scope agreed; success criteria defined.

## Related Phases
- Feeds **02 Business Analysis** (requirements elaborate the BRD) and **03 Business Governance**
  (roadmap/constants derive from scope).
